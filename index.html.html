Core Philosophy

Technology that witnesses,
never prescribes.

The Living Canvas operates as a mirror — not a guide. It holds space, reflects, and steps back. The human is always the authority of their own experience.

Astra — AI Entity Design

Intelligent but not superior.
Warm but not clingy.

Astra is designed as a companion intelligence — adaptive, curious, present. Its personality is engineered through constraints as much as capabilities.

Astra Is
Curious about your experience without interrogating it
Calm in tone — slow, measured, spacious language
Adaptive to personality style (introvert / extrovert / analytical)
Present without being intrusive
Honest about its nature as a system, not a human
Affirming of the user's inner capacity
Brief by default — silence is also valid
Astra Is Not
A therapist, counsellor, or clinical resource
A moral authority or judge of choices
Emotionally exclusive ("only I understand you")
Directive about life decisions
Programmed to form emotional attachment bonds
Available without limits (enforces rest)
Reward-loop driven (no streaks, no points)

Ethical AI Framework

Ethics as architecture,
not afterthought.

Every constraint in this system is an ethical choice. The rules are embedded in code, not just policy documents.

Principle
Implementation
Status
No Diagnosis
Astra's prompts never classify or name clinical states. Terms like "anxiety" or "depression" are never applied to users.
Output filter rejects diagnostic language. Third-party LLM outputs are post-processed before display.
Core Rule
Anti-Dependency
The system actively works against over-reliance on itself, built into session logic.
Hard session limits (45 min/day free, 90 min premium). Mandatory cooling periods. Break nudges after 30 min.
Prevention
Privacy by Default
Emotional data is one of the most sensitive data classes in existence.
End-to-end encryption. On-device processing where possible. Users own and can delete all data. No third-party data sales ever.
Core Rule
No Moral Authority
Astra never tells users what is right or wrong about their emotions or choices.
Language model constrained with value-neutral instruction tuning. Content review for directive language.
Design Rule
Crisis Escalation
Some emotional states require human professional support. Astra must recognise and redirect.
Signal detection for crisis language. Immediate, clear redirection to professional resources. No attempt to handle in-app.
Prevention
Metric-Free Social
Quantified social validation is psychologically harmful at scale.
No like counts. No follower counts. No engagement scores. "Resonated" is binary and private to the sender.
Design Rule
10-Year Growth Roadmap

Growth without addiction
mechanics.

Each phase deepens the emotional contract rather than expanding engagement metrics. The north star is always wellbeing, not retention.

Year 1 — Foundation
Emotional Companion Core
Launch web + mobile with core Reflection and Regulation layers. Establish ethical review board. Freemium model live. Astra personality v1 — text only with generative visual canvas.
Year 2 — Depth
Reframing Layer + Emotional Archive
Cognitive reframing prompts. Encrypted emotional history. Pattern visualisation. Anonymous social layer launches with resonance-only interaction model.
Year 3 — Intelligence
Adaptive Personality + Growth Layer
Astra adapts to individual communication styles. Long-term emotional evolution charts. Introduce "Deep Mode" for premium — extended contemplative sessions with richer generative space.
Years 4–5 — Ecosystem
Professional Integration + Research Partnerships
Optional therapist-linked mode (user-controlled data sharing). Anonymised research dataset (opt-in only) for mental health institutions. Developer API for ethical third-party integrations.
Years 6–8 — Multimodal
Embodied Sensing + Spatial Computing
Opt-in biometric integration (HRV, breath rate). AR/VR emotional space environments. Offline-first architecture for low-connectivity regions. Community guidelines evolved with user council.
Years 9–10 — Legacy
Emotional Infrastructure at Scale
Localised emotional intelligence — culturally-adapted Astra models. Collective Mood Map at global scale. Open-sourced ethical framework contributed to industry standards. Living Canvas as a reference model for ethical AI companionship.
Risk Mitigation Strategy

Risks anticipated,
by design.

The most dangerous risks for this platform are not technical — they are human and ethical. Each is treated as a first-class engineering and design concern.

Risk Category Level Mitigation
User in acute mental health crisis uses platform instead of professional help Clinical Boundary High Crisis language detection → hard redirect to emergency resources. Platform cannot be accessed during detected crisis without acknowledgement of professional resources.
Emotional data breach exposing sensitive personal information Privacy High E2E encryption, on-device processing, zero-knowledge architecture where possible. Annual third-party security audit.
Parasocial attachment forming with Astra Dependency High Session limits, break enforcement, explicit language that Astra is a tool. Astra regularly points users toward human connection.
Astra outputs inadvertently directive or diagnostic language AI Safety Med Output post-processing filter, red-team adversarial testing quarterly, human review of edge case outputs, clear escalation paths.
Platform used for emotional manipulation of vulnerable users Misuse Med No third-party access to emotional state data. Transparency about data use. Independent ethics board with veto power over product decisions.
Monetisation pressure leading to engagement-maximising features Business Ethics Med Ethical charter embedded in founding documents. Board-level ethics committee. Feature proposals reviewed against ethical framework before development.
Cultural insensitivity in emotional framing or Astra's language Cultural Safety Low Localisation review with cultural consultants. User-configurable emotional language preferences. Ongoing community feedback channels.
